Overview

The BCCP and IAS are happy to announce the summer workshop: Šmartno 2022 !
After a long break due to Covid, we are excited to bring together again in person a selected group of experts on reconstruction, nonlinear modeling, simulations and observations. Our workshop will be highly interactive and based on facilitating discussions among the participants, supplemented by talks, discussion sessions, complemented by excursions to the vineyards in the hills surrounding the picturesque village of Šmartno.

The conference venue: Hotel St. Martin
